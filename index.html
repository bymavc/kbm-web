ABOUT KBM

KBM stands for Knowledge Base Manager and is a system focused to build and manage knowledge bases for workgroups, allowing them to gives the necesary access priviledges and make its content either public or private as wish

FEATURES

Look at what you can do with KBM!

Create Bases to Share Content

Create knowledge bases to work with your workgroups or share structured information with your friends. Remember that your bases can be public or private, it's up to you!

Choose your collaborators

Feel free to add collaborators to help with your knowledge bases. You can add Workers to create content or readers to see it if your bases are private.

Download as PDF

You can download all the content on your knowledge bases using the download button available on the top tight of the document layout.
